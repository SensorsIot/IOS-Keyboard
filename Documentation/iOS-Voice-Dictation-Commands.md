## Tips

1. **Auto-Punctuation**: iOS 16+ automatically adds periods, commas, and question marks based on your speech patterns
2. **Seamless Switching**: Tap screen to type, then just speak to resume dictation
3. **Quiet Environment**: Works best in quiet places
4. **30-Second Timeout**: Dictation stops after 30 seconds of silence
5. **Corrections**: Tap underlined words to see spelling alternatives
